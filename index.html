Nemanja Bartolović

Software Engineer @ Meta

Zurich, Switzerland

Hi there! I’m a software engineer with an interest in real-time rendering and adjacent fields. I’m currently working at Meta where I am focusing on efficient photorealistic rendering methods via 3D reconstruction for mobile devices.

I am the lead developer of our cross-platform, in-house 3D Gaussian splatting engine that powers both scenes (Hyperscapes) and Codec Avatars, written in Vulkan.

If you have a Quest device, you can try our Hyperscape Horizon Demo to see some of reconstructed environments in VR!
