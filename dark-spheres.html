Dark Spheres Concept UI

This solo project was created for the course "Human Computer Interaction" during my Bachelor's Degree at Faculty of Electronic Engineering, University of Nis.

The main idea was the showcase UI design patterns commonly found in (MMO)RP games, as well as the flexibilty and rapid prototyping in QML. The UI developed is reminiscent of MMOs popular in the beginning of 2010s.
